      Templates:
      Our coding team does its best to set up our templates and skins for all the lastest desktop browsers. We do our best to check out creations on mobile devices but with the vast amount of operating systems and browsers we can not offer support for all mobile devices. Before purchasing a template, please take a few minutes to navigate the site and let your owners navigate it as well. We do have some reports that some mobile devices and older versions of IE do not play well with some designs. We will not recode our templates for compatibility with random browsers or mobile devices. We do have full support for operation on Firefox, Chrome, IE and Safari , provided your using the latest versions of those browsers on desktops or laptop computers.

If your graphic requires more revisions there may be an additional fee required to finish the project.
